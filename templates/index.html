Research Question: Do UFO Sightings happen more frequently in a certain time of a year?

A certain time can refer to a particular date, month or season.

I first examined the original UFO Sightings map. I noticed that information including the event name, time, location, description and number of close encounters is available for a total of 100 sightings. However, 37 sightings' time data is only precise to the year level while the remaining is precise to the month or date level.

For the 63 sightings with month data, I labeled each one with the month they took place in and changed the marker icon accordingly. Number 1 - 10 represents months January to October, Letter N represents November and Letter D represents December. The visual representation shows that November and December seem to more UFO sightings than other months. I plot a histogram chart to better display the number of sightings each month.
